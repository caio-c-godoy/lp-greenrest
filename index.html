Ten carefully engineered layers working together to align your spine, relieve pressure and keep your back supported all night — not just “more foam”.

Performance Knit Cover

Soft, breathable top fabric with performance yarns that gently reflect body heat as soothing energy, helping circulation and all-night comfort.

Flame-retardant protection without fiberglass or harsh chemical sprays, so you get safety without sacrificing health.

3

Bio-Ceramic Comfort Film

A thin bio-ceramic layer that helps regulate temperature and supports deep, restorative sleep while keeping the surface smooth.

4

3D ConvoTech™ Cool-Gel Layer

“Egg-crate” 3D design with cooling gel that reduces pressure points, increases airflow and helps keep your back and hips cooler.

5

Pressure-Relief Latex

Organic latex that adapts to your body curves, cradling shoulders and hips while keeping your lower back supported.

6

Responsive Support Layer

Zoned support that helps align your spine for different body weights and shapes, so you don’t sink where you need structure.

7

Intelligent Support Core – Part A

High-density orthopedic foam that adds stability, durability and even support from head to toe.

8

Shock-Absorbing Transition Layer

Absorbs impact and movement so you feel less of your partner’s tossing and turning and wake up with fewer micro-awakenings.

9

Intelligent Support Core – Part B

Reinforced base foam that completes the orthopedic structure and keeps the mattress from sagging over time.

10

Stabilizing Foundation & Edge Support

Structural base that improves edge support, makes it easier to get in and out of bed and keeps every layer above working in alignment.
